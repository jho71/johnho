About Me

I am a candidate for the Bachelor of Technology in Software Development at Seneca College.

The day I bought my first phone, I came to the realization of how heavily integrated software was in our daily lives; it controls our daily essentials: online banking, communication, and most importantly to my sixteen-year-old self, video games. In high school, I took multiple programming courses, which inspired me to make my own programs. After graduating, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in software development, which leads me to where I am today, as a skilled, hardworking, and dedicated software developer.
