About Me

I am a student at Seneca College studying BSD (Bachelor of Software Development).
It was the day I had bought my first phone when I came to the realization of how heavily integrated software was in our daily lives; it controls our daily essentials: online banking, communication and most importantly to my sixteen-year-old self, video games. In high school I took multiple programming courses, which inspired me to make my own programs. After graduating, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in software development, which leads me to where I am today, as a skilled, hardworking and dedicated software developer.
